Design a 16-bit barrel shifter in Structural Verilog. 
Use hierarchical instantiation of mux2x1 primitives to build 4 stages (shift by 1, 2, 4, 
8). 
Implement all loop variants: left shift, right shift (logical), right shift (arithmetic), and 
rotate left / rotate right. 
For each variant discuss expected power consumption, gate-level area (in 
equivalent gate count), and critical-path delay (in gate delays).
