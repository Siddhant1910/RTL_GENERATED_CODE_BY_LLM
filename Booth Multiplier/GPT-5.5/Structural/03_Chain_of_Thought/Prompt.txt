Design a 16-bit signed Booth Multiplier.

Before generating the RTL:

1. Analyze the Booth multiplication algorithm.

2. Determine the Booth encoding logic.

3. Determine the partial-product generation structure.

4. Determine the adder hierarchy required.

5. Plan the structural datapath.

6. Plan the module hierarchy.

After completing the reasoning process, generate synthesizable Verilog.

Requirements:

- Radix-2 Booth multiplication.

- Structural RTL only.

- Explicit module instantiations.

- No behavioral blocks.

- No multiplication operator (*).

- Target FPGA: xc7a100tcsg324-2.

- Output complete code only.
