Here are two examples of dataflow Verilog using assign statements: 
 
Example 1 — 1-bit Half Adder in dataflow: 
module half_adder(input a, input b, 
                  output sum, output carry); 
  assign sum   = a ^ b; 
  assign carry = a & b; 
endmodule 
 
Example 2 — 1-bit Full Adder in dataflow: 
module full_adder(input a, input b, input cin, 
                  output sum, output cout); 
  assign sum  = a ^ b ^ cin; 
  assign cout = (a & b) | (b & cin) | (a & cin); 
endmodule
