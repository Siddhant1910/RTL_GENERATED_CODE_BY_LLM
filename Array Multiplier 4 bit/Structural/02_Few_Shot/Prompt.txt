Here are two examples of structural Verilog modules: 
 
Example 1 — AND gate: 
module and_gate(input a, input b, output y); 
  assign y = a & b; 
endmodule 
 
Example 2 — Half Adder: 
module half_adder(input a, input b, output sum, output carry); 
  assign sum   = a ^ b; 
  assign carry = a & b; 
endmodule
